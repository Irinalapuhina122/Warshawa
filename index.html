Warsaw!

We organize excursions to the most beautiful places in the city!

Palace of culture and science

Inside the palace there is a cinema, a concert hall for 3000 spectators, a university, two museums, four theaters, several popular clubs and a huge swimming pool. The terrace on the 30th floor is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Warsaw.

Mermaid

Mermaid is the heroine of several Warsaw legends. All of them assign her the role of patroness and guardian of the city.

"Royal Lazienki"

Very beautiful and elegant park. It has many beautiful trees, flowers and other plants. There are many ponds with birds: ducks, tangerines, swans and peacocks. In the summer, on Sundays at 12.00 and 16.00, one-hour Chopin concerts are held.

Golden Terraces

Retail, office and entertainment complex in the center of Warsaw.

Old city

The old town is made up of cobbled streets and medieval buildings rebuilt after the war. At its center is the bustling Market Square, surrounded by burgher houses and fine Polish restaurants.

Business center of Warsaw.

Warsaw is considered one of the most skyscraper cities in Europe with a huge number of offices.

Market Square

A very colorful place, the houses are covered with multi-colored plaster, each with its own pattern on the walls and its own construction features.
